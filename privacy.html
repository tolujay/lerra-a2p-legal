MOBILE COMPLIANCE AND SMS PRIVACY POLICY

  1. Information Collection: We collect mobile phone numbers explicitly provided by users during account registration, checkout, or account recovery tools on our platform.
  2. Use of Mobile Data: Your mobile number is used strictly to transmit requested transactional messages, account security verification codes (OTP), and account recovery instructions.
  3. Absolute Privacy: No mobile information or phone numbers collected for SMS messaging purposes will be shared, sold, rented, or leased with third parties, affiliates, or partners for marketing or promotional purposes under any circumstances.
  4. Opt-Out & Support: Users can text "STOP" at any time to opt-out of all future text communications. Text "HELP" for customer support instructions, or contact us directly at support.Lerra@shopLerra.com. Message and data rates may apply.
